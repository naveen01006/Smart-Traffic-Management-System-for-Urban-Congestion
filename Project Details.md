### Project Title

**Smart Traffic Management System for Urban Congestion Using Artificial Intelligence and IoT**

---

### Problem Statement

Urban areas are experiencing severe traffic congestion due to the rapid increase in the number of vehicles, inefficient traffic signal control, and lack of real-time traffic monitoring. Traffic congestion leads to increased travel time, fuel consumption, air pollution, and road accidents. Traditional traffic management systems operate on fixed signal timings and cannot adapt to changing traffic conditions. Therefore, there is a need for a smart traffic management system that can monitor traffic density in real time and dynamically control traffic signals to reduce congestion and improve traffic flow.

---

### Project Objectives

1. To monitor real-time traffic conditions using cameras and sensors.
2. To analyze traffic density using Artificial Intelligence techniques.
3. To optimize traffic signal timings based on vehicle density.
4. To reduce traffic congestion and waiting time at intersections.
5. To improve road safety and traffic efficiency.
6. To provide traffic information for better urban transportation planning.

---
